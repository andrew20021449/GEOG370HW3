Projections

Creating different map projections in QGIS is simple! After importing the globe vector and installing the Indicatrix Mapper plugin, all that's needed to create each different map is to change the projection used in the bottom right corner! For this project, I used a lot of common projections, including WGS84 and Pseudo-Mercator, as well as some less common ones, including Adams Square and Azimuthal. I hope you enjoy!
